Anime Recommendations

The table below contains my recommendations and links to the to Crunchyroll page of each anime. Each of these shows can be viewed for free if you your research ;)

Anime Title Image Reason for Recommendation

Demon Slayer

Adventure-Dark Fantasy-Martial Arts

 Demon slayer was won of my favorite manga and since its animated by Ufotable the anime is the potential to outdue its manga counterpart. This anime is about a boy named Tanjiro whose on a quest kill demons and turn his sister back into a human. Its action packed and has a good balance of serious and funny moments.

Jobless Reincarnation

Fantasy-Isekai

 Jobless Reinacaration is probably my favorite anime at them moment. It has a very interesting and unpredictable story set in a very mysterious and magicial world. If you enjoy adventure and action you should consider giving this a try.

Parasyte -the maxim-

Horror-Thriller-Science Fiction

 Parayte is one of my favorite anime even thought it makes me sad to think about it. It's full of suspense, action, an mystery.

To Your Eternity

Fantasy-Super Natural

 To Your Eternity is one of my favorite anime of all time but also one of the saddest I've seen. Fushi is a being who is learning to become human through meeting friend and foes. If you enjoy mystery, action, and suspenseful anime, this is the show for you.
