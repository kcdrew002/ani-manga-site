Manga Recommendations

The table below contains my recommendations and links to the to Shonen Jump page of each manga. Each of these manga can be viewed for free if you your research ;)

Manga Image Reason for Recommendation

Chainsaw Man

Action-Comedy-Horror

 I read this manga last spring in my dorm room and count stop once I started. Chainsaw Man is extremely engaging and great if you enjoy a little gore and comedy in your manga.

Dandadan

Action-Romatic Comedy-
Supernatural Thriller

 As of right now I'm about 30 chapters in to this manga so I'm about halfway through, even though there are still chapters releasing daily. This manga is just really funny overall and has amazing action panels and a storyline to back it up.

Mashle: Muscles and Magic

Adventure-Fantasy Comedy

 The way I like to think of this manga is like a Siatama from One Punch Man enrolled in Hogwarts. Mash uses his pure athleticism to defeat enemies with immense magical capabilies. This manga a good mix of comedy and action and is getting an anime adaptation in 2023.

The Promised Neverland

Dark Fantasy-Science Fiction-
Thriller

 The manga is about a group of orphans trying to escape and survive in a world of demons. It's full of suspense and msytery and was one of the first manga I've completed.
